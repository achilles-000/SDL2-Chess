CHESS PIECE IMAGES
==================

Place your chess piece images in this folder with the following naming convention:

WHITE PIECES:
- white_pawn.png
- white_rook.png
- white_knight.png
- white_bishop.png
- white_queen.png
- white_king.png

BLACK PIECES:
- black_pawn.png
- black_rook.png
- black_knight.png
- black_bishop.png
- black_queen.png
- black_king.png

IMAGE REQUIREMENTS:
- Format: PNG or JPG (PNG recommended for transparency)
- Recommended size: 64x64 to 128x128 pixels
- The images will be automatically scaled to fit the squares

If images are not found, the game will fall back to Unicode chess symbols or simple shapes.

You can download chess piece images from:
- https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Chess_pieces
- https://opengameart.org/ (search for "chess pieces")
- Or create your own!
